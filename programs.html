COMMUNITY ART CENTER

During my Junior year of high school, the Community Art Center was an art program that I went to to learn more about art. In the program, our group was commissioned by the city of Cambridge to create a mural on a building in Kendall Square. We were commissioned to make the building look less dull in the area it was located in. Each of the students in our program were required to create an artwork that would help represent The Port, a place in Cambridge, then, we would all of our artwork into a collage. Here is my artwork that I did for the collage and our collage on the building in Kendall Square.
